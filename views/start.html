Use this service to appoint or remove (terminate the appointment of) a director of a limited company.

You can use this service to:

  • appoint a new director – for now, you can only appoint a director who is a person
  • remove a director – you can remove a director who is a person or a corporate director

To appoint a corporate director, you'll need to use our WebFiling service or file a Companies House form.
